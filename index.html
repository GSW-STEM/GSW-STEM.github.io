About the Initiative

The Brazilian NGO D3S Consultoria and the South African New Innovations Consulting and Projects (NICP) invite women engineers, computer scientists, data scientists, AI specialists and STEM leaders from the Global South to connect, collaborate and co-create the future.

By combining our international networks, we exchange experiences, discuss shared challenges, and develop strategies to position our communities for the decades ahead.

Our Goals

• Empower those who empower
• Grow the Global South tech space from bottom-up
• Women-centered exchange of strategies and leadership
• Strengthen global positioning and technological autonomy

Key Challenges

• Lack of education, rights and health access
• Infrastructure dependence on developed countries
• Limited knowledge of international power structures
• Digital positioning and representation
• Cross-government collaboration gaps
• Sustainable technologies and energy transitions
